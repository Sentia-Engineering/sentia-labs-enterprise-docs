The Resource Library has two tabs:

| Tab | Use it for |
| --- | --- |
| **My research** | Upload and manage source research artifacts that can ground cohorts. |
| **Reports** | Revisit saved simulation and comparison reports. |

It is the evidence shelf for the workspace: raw research comes in, simulation evidence goes out, and both stay tied to the team.

## My research

Upload research artifacts when they should inform future cohorts or analysis:

- Interview transcripts.
- Survey CSV/TSV/DAT files.
- Persona documents.
- PDFs, TXT, and DOCX research notes.
- Audio or video recordings that should be transcribed.
- Past A/B or conversion history files.

Uploaded files are parsed into structured research memory. The file remains visible in the library with status, summary, metadata, and actions such as rename, download, re-parse, and delete.

## Reports

Reports are saved from:

- A completed simulation result.
- A simulation comparison.
- A PDF export flow with save enabled.

Reports can be private or workspace-visible, depending on the save action and your role. Admins can review wider report scopes for workspace operations.

## Where the library fits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Before a study">
    Upload source research so cohorts can reflect real user language and evidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="During cohort creation">
    Attach relevant research artifacts from the Population Composer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="After a study">
    Save the simulation or comparison report so the team can revisit the evidence later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
