The **Decisions log** at `/decisions` is the source of truth for everything your team has evaluated with Sentia. Every simulation creates a decision; every shipped decision becomes a calibration event.

## What's in the log

Each decision shows:

- **Question** — what was being evaluated
- **State** — current lifecycle stage (Proposed / Simulated / Shipped / Validated / Reverted)
- **Owner** — the teammate who initiated it
- **Simulation summary** — distribution, confidence, headline prediction
- **Calibration grade** — predicted vs. actual once shipped (if applicable)
- **Linked artifacts** — Linear/Jira issue, Figma frames, PRDs, related decisions

## Filters

- **State** — narrow by lifecycle stage
- **Owner** — your decisions, your team's, all
- **Time window** — last 7d / 30d / 90d / custom
- **Confidence range** — find high- or low-confidence decisions
- **Calibration grade** — find decisions where Sentia was very accurate or notably off
- **Tag** — custom tags (e.g., `pricing`, `onboarding`, `enterprise`)

`/` focuses search; `J`/`K` navigate rows; `Enter` opens the decision detail page.

## What the log is for

Three real uses:

## Decision detail page

Click any decision row to open `/decisions/[id]`:

- **Header** — question, hypothesis, success criteria, owner, state, tags
- **Linked simulation(s)** — every simulation run for this decision (some decisions get re-simulated as scope changes)
- **Calibration** — actuals once shipped, predicted vs. actual delta, accuracy grade
- **Linked artifacts** — issues, frames, PRDs auto-pulled from your connected tools
- **Activity** — full history of state transitions with timestamps and actors
- **Comments** — team annotations (great for retros)

## Custom tags

Add tags to organize:

- By domain — `pricing`, `onboarding`, `notifications`, `mobile`
- By campaign — `q2-launches`, `migration-2026`
- By outcome — `revenue-positive`, `npsneutral`
- By learning — `model-was-wrong`, `surprised-by-result`

Tags are workspace-scoped. You can filter and report on them.

## Exporting

Decisions log exports:

- **CSV** — for analysis in spreadsheets
- **PDF** — formatted report for quarterly business reviews
- **JSON** — via API at `GET /decisions` — for piping into your own tooling

See the [API reference](/api-reference/introduction).
