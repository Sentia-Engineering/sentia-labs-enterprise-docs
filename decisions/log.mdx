The **Decisions log** is an advanced release-loop surface for workspaces that connect simulations to shipped outcomes and calibration. It is not required for first-week design research onboarding, where most teams live in Design Agent, Simulations, Populations, and the Resource Library.

## What's in the log

Each decision shows:

- **Question** ‚Äî what was being evaluated
- **State** ‚Äî current lifecycle stage (Proposed / Simulated / Shipped / Validated / Reverted)
- **Owner** ‚Äî the teammate who initiated it
- **Simulation summary** ‚Äî distribution, confidence, headline prediction
- **Calibration grade** ‚Äî predicted vs. actual once shipped (if applicable)
- **Linked artifacts** ‚Äî Linear/Jira issue, Figma frames, PRDs, related decisions

## Filters

- **State** ‚Äî narrow by lifecycle stage
- **Owner** ‚Äî your decisions, your team's, all
- **Time window** ‚Äî last 7d / 30d / 90d / custom
- **Confidence range** ‚Äî find high- or low-confidence decisions
- **Calibration grade** ‚Äî find decisions where Sentia was very accurate or notably off
- **Tag** ‚Äî custom tags (e.g., `pricing`, `onboarding`, `enterprise`)

`/` focuses search; `J`/`K` navigate rows; `Enter` opens the decision detail page.

## Decision detail page

Click any decision row to open `/decisions/[id]`:

- **Header** ‚Äî question, hypothesis, success criteria, owner, state, tags
- **Linked simulation(s)** ‚Äî every simulation run for this decision (some decisions get re-simulated as scope changes)
- **Calibration** ‚Äî actuals once shipped, predicted vs. actual delta, accuracy grade
- **Linked artifacts** ‚Äî issues, frames, PRDs auto-pulled from your connected tools
- **Activity** ‚Äî full history of state transitions with timestamps and actors
- **Comments** ‚Äî team annotations (great for retros)

## Custom tags

Add tags to organize:

- By domain ‚Äî `pricing`, `onboarding`, `notifications`, `mobile`
- By campaign ‚Äî `q2-launches`, `migration-2026`
- By outcome ‚Äî `revenue-positive`, `npsneutral`
- By learning ‚Äî `model-was-wrong`, `surprised-by-result`

Tags are workspace-scoped. You can filter and report on them.

## Exporting

Decisions log exports:

- **CSV** ‚Äî for analysis in spreadsheets
- **PDF** ‚Äî formatted report for quarterly business reviews
- **JSON** ‚Äî via API at `GET /decisions` ‚Äî for piping into your own tooling

See the [API reference](/api-reference/introduction).
