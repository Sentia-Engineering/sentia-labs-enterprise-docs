This quickstart gets you to your first simulation result fast. You'll connect one analytics provider, use a Sentia-provided sample population, and run a short simulation against a real scenario.

<Note>
  **Prerequisites:** Your workspace owner has finished SSO setup and invited you. If you don't have an invite, ping your CSM or [support@sentialabs.ai](mailto:support@sentialabs.ai).
</Note>

## Step 1 · Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Visit [app.sentialabs.ai](https://app.sentialabs.ai). You'll be redirected to your corporate identity provider (Okta, Entra ID, Google Workspace, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on Simulations">
    After SSO, you'll land on `/simulations`. The left nav is your map; press `?` any time to see all keyboard shortcuts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Sentia is **desktop-first** and tuned for screens ≥1024px. Most workflows are also fully keyboard-driven — try `S`, `P`, `D`, `I`, `C` to jump between sections.
</Tip>

## Step 2 · Connect one analytics provider

Sentia needs at least one analytics source to ground agents in real usage data. Pick **one** of Amplitude, Mixpanel, or PostHog (these are mutually exclusive — one per workspace).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Press `I` or click **Integrations** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your provider">
    Click the **Connect** button on your provider card. For Amplitude/Mixpanel you'll paste an API key pair; for PostHog you'll paste a personal API key and host URL.
    
    See the per-provider setup guides: [Amplitude](/integrations/analytics#amplitude), [Mixpanel](/integrations/analytics#mixpanel), [PostHog](/integrations/analytics#posthog).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for first sync">
    Initial sync runs immediately and typically completes in 5–15 minutes for cohorts, funnels, and event frequency. You'll see a green **Connected** badge with last-sync time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Don't connect a second analytics provider — they're mutually exclusive by design so agent memory stays consistent. If you need to switch, contact support.
</Warning>

## Step 3 · Use the sample population

Every new workspace ships with a **Sample US Census Population (1,000 agents)** so you can run a simulation before building your own.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Populations">
    Press `P` to navigate. You should see the sample population card with **1,000 agents**, a segment breakdown chart, and a green **Validated** badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Inspect it">
    Click the card to see the demographic mix, adopter category distribution (Innovator → Laggard), and the behavioral validation report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You'll build a custom, interview-grounded population in the onboarding flow. For your first run, the sample is enough to see results end-to-end. See [Build a population](/populations/build) when you're ready.
</Tip>

## Step 4 · Run your first simulation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the wizard">
    Press `N` (or click **New simulation**). The 4-step wizard opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision (Step 1)">
    Choose a **proposed product change** to evaluate. For your first run, pick a built-in template — for example, *"Reduce free trial from 14 days to 7 days"*. The template prefills the scenario, success criteria, and a sample analytics filter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scenario (Step 2)">
    Add a **visual input**. The fastest first-run option is **Text only** — paste a 2–3 sentence description of the change. (Captured UI is ~15–20% more accurate; you'll learn that in [Inputs](/simulations/inputs).)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Population (Step 3)">
    Select the **Sample US Census Population**. Sentia auto-suggests segments to focus on; leave the defaults.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure (Step 4)">
    Pick **Fast mode (200 agents)** for your first run. It converges in 2–3 waves and finishes in ~3–5 minutes. Click **Launch simulation** (or `⌘⇧L`).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5 · Watch it run

You'll land on the live run page. A few things to watch:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Agent counter" icon="users">
    Updates every 10 agents. Announced via `aria-live` for screen readers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Convergence chart" icon="chart-line">
    Jensen-Shannon divergence across waves. Two consecutive stable waves trigger convergence.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Distribution chart" icon="chart-bar">
    Real-time choice distribution. Watch how it stabilizes as agents accumulate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live transcripts" icon="message">
    Tail individual agent reasoning as the simulation runs. Pause anytime.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step 6 · Read your results

When the run completes, you'll see the **8-tab results view**:

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Overview** | Headline distribution, confidence score, top concerns, NPS prediction |
| **Recommendations** | Sentia-generated actions to address objections and amplify positives |
| **Segments** | Breakdown by adopter category, persona, plan tier, tenure |
| **Objections** | Top concern themes — clustered customer-verbatim style |
| **Agents** | Individual agent decisions, sortable and filterable |
| **Transcripts** | Full agent reasoning chains for any agent |
| **Adoption Cascade** | Innovator → Laggard adoption curve over a 12-month horizon |
| **Calibration** | Predicted vs. actual once you've shipped the feature |

<Tip>
  Press `1`–`8` to switch tabs. Press `⌘E` to export the report as PDF for your stakeholders.
</Tip>

## You're done

That's your first simulation. From here, you have three productive next steps:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Build a real population" icon="users-gear" href="/populations/build">
    Replace the sample with one grounded in your analytics + interview transcripts. Aim for ~85% accuracy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wire up auto-triggers" icon="bolt" href="/simulations/triggers">
    Auto-simulate when Linear/Jira issues hit "In Review" or a Figma frame is marked "simulate".
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connect ship detection" icon="rotate" href="/calibration/overview">
    Connect LaunchDarkly, GitHub, or Statsig/Eppo so Sentia learns from every release.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  **Stuck or surprised by a result?** Your CSM is the fastest channel — they have visibility into your workspace and can review the run with you. For technical issues, [support@sentialabs.ai](mailto:support@sentialabs.ai) or the status page at [status.sentialabs.ai](https://status.sentialabs.ai).
</Note>
