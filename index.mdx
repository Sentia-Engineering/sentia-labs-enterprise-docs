Sentia is a **behavioral product simulation platform** built for enterprise product, research, and design teams. Connect your stack, define a decision, and a population of synthetic agents — grounded in census data, behavioral science, and your real customer verbatims — predicts how your users will react before you ship.

## How Sentia works

Every agent in a simulation runs through a three-step decision chain:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Memory" icon="brain">
    The agent recalls its behavioral profile, usage history, and real customer verbatims from your connected tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reflection" icon="lightbulb">
    The agent reasons about the scenario, weighing its history, preferences, and concerns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision" icon="check">
    The agent commits to a choice with a confidence score, rationale, concerns, and an NPS prediction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Results converge through wave-based sampling. With interview-grounded populations, Sentia achieves **~85% normalized accuracy** vs. ~70% for demographic-only agents.
