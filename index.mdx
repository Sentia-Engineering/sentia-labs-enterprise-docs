## How Sentia works

Every agent in a simulation runs through a three-step reasoning chain:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Memory" icon="brain">
    The agent recalls its demographic profile, behavioral traits, research memories, and relevant cohort context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reflection" icon="lightbulb">
    The agent reasons about the scenario, weighing its history, preferences, and concerns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision" icon="check">
    The agent commits to a choice with a confidence score, rationale, concerns, and an NPS prediction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Results stream live, then compile into a decision report designed for critique, prioritization, and stakeholder review.
