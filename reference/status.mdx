## What this page covers

This page reflects the customer-visible state of Sentia production services. A dedicated real-time status dashboard with incident history and subscriptions is coming soon — until then, this page is the source of truth and is updated by Sentia operations during incidents.

For workspace-specific incidents, your Slack Connect channel and CSM remain the fastest path.

## Reporting an issue

| Type | Where to send it |
| --- | --- |
| **Errors or unexpected behavior** | [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) |
| **Production outage / SEV-1** | [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[SUPPORT]` + your Slack Connect channel |
| **Security incident** | [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[SECURITY]` |

When reporting an error, include:

- Workspace slug
- Time window (UTC)
- What you were trying to do and what you saw
- Affected simulation, population, or integration ID if applicable
