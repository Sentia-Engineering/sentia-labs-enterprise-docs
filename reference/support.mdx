Sentia is an enterprise platform with dedicated support. All email goes to a single inbox — [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) — and is routed internally by subject-line prefix. This page covers the channels, what they're for, and the response SLAs.

## Channels — by intent

### Day-to-day questions, onboarding, training

**Your CSM (Customer Success Manager)**

Your CSM is the primary channel for:

- Onboarding help (week-one through year-one)
- Best-practice questions
- Population strategy
- Calibration interpretation
- Roadmap and feature requests
- Contract and commercial questions

Slack Connect channel set up at kickoff is the fastest path. Email backup: your CSM's direct email.

### Technical issues, bugs, incidents

Email [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[SUPPORT]`.

For:

- Bugs and unexpected behavior
- Integration failures
- SSO / SCIM issues
- Performance degradation
- Data sync problems
- API errors

Email creates a tracked ticket with our 24/7 engineering on-call rotation. Include:

- Workspace slug
- Time window (UTC)
- Affected simulation/population/integration ID
- Browser network trace or HAR file if applicable
- What you were trying to do and what happened

### Security incidents and vulnerability reports

Email [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[SECURITY]`.

For:

- Suspected security incident in your workspace
- Vulnerability report (responsible disclosure)
- Compliance questions (SOC 2, GDPR, sub-processors)
- DPA requests
- Pen-test reports

PGP key available on request. Response within 1 business day; immediate for critical reports.

### Procurement, contracts, vendor management

Email [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[PROCUREMENT]`.

For:

- Vendor onboarding paperwork
- Insurance certificates
- W-9 / W-8 / VAT registration
- Renewal logistics
- MSA / DPA negotiation

### Privacy-specific requests

Email [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) with subject prefix `[PRIVACY]`.

For:

- GDPR right-to-erasure requests
- Right-to-portability data exports
- Customer privacy-policy clarifications
- Data subject access requests

## Response SLAs

| Request type | Initial response | Resolution target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **CSM (Slack Connect)** | Same business day | Per request scope |
| **`[SUPPORT]`** — SEV-1 (outage) | 15 min | < 4h, with hourly updates |
| **`[SUPPORT]`** — SEV-2 (major degradation) | 30 min | < 8h |
| **`[SUPPORT]`** — SEV-3 (single integration / minor) | 2 hours | < 2 business days |
| **`[SUPPORT]`** — SEV-4 (non-urgent) | 1 business day | Per backlog priority |
| **`[SECURITY]`** | 1 business day; immediate for critical | Per scope |
| **`[PRIVACY]`** | 1 business day | GDPR SLAs |
| **`[PROCUREMENT]`** | 1 business day | Per request |

## Status & incident communication

| Surface | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| **[Service status](/reference/status)** | Current state of production services and where to report errors |
| **Slack Connect channel** | Real-time updates for incidents affecting your workspace |
| **In-app banner** | Workspace-affecting service degradations |
| **Email** — for SEV-1 only | Customer-affecting outages > 30 min |

For now, the Slack Connect channel and email are the primary notification surfaces; a subscribable real-time status dashboard is coming soon.

## Office hours

| Cadence | Audience | Format |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Weekly customer office hours** | Open to all customers | 30-min open Q&A; Tue 11am PT / 8pm CET |
| **Monthly product roadmap** | Customers + observers | 45-min preview of upcoming releases; first Tue of month |
| **Quarterly business review (QBR)** | Per workspace | 90-min strategic review with your CSM |

Office hours are recorded and shared in the Slack Connect channel.

## Feature requests

Three paths:

1. **CSM-routed** — discuss with your CSM; high-impact requests get prioritized
2. **In-app feedback** — `?` overlay → "Send feedback" — goes straight to product team
3. **Customer advisory board** — quarterly invitation-only sessions for design partners

The product roadmap is shared in monthly roadmap sessions. We don't publish dated commitments publicly.

## Training & enablement

- **Onboarding training** — included in kickoff; 2-hour live session for your first cohort
- **Refresher training** — quarterly, opt-in; covers new features and best practices
- **Custom training** — paid; deep-dive sessions for advanced topics (calibration strategy, integration architecture, etc.)

Discuss training needs with your CSM.

## Severity definitions (for context)

| Level | Definition | Response time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SEV-1** | Platform down, all simulations failing | 15 min |
| **SEV-2** | Major feature degraded, > 10% error rate | 30 min |
| **SEV-3** | Minor degradation, single integration down | 2 hours |
| **SEV-4** | Non-urgent, no customer impact | Next business day |

## Anti-patterns

A few patterns we ask customers to avoid:

- **Don't paste credentials into support emails.** Use the secure-share link Sentia ops sends instead.
- **Don't share workspace export bundles outside your IT/security team.** They contain audit-loggable customer activity.
- **Don't open multiple tickets for the same issue.** Reply to your existing thread; we route accordingly.
- **For SEV-1, email [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io) even if you also ping the CSM Slack channel.** Email triggers the on-call rotation; Slack alone might not.
