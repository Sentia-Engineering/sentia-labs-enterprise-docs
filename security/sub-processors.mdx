A **sub-processor** is a third-party service that processes customer data on Sentia's behalf. Sentia maintains a current list and notifies customers 30 days in advance of any sub-processor changes.

This list applies to both US and EU workspaces unless noted.

## Current sub-processors

### Infrastructure & hosting

| Sub-processor | Service | Region | Data processed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** | Compute, storage, networking, KMS, Secrets Manager, GuardDuty | US workspaces: us-east-1, us-west-2. EU workspaces: eu-west-1, eu-central-1. | All categories — primary infrastructure |
| **Render** | Production hosting for application services | US: Oregon. EU: Frankfurt. | Application logs (no customer content), environment configuration |
| **Aiven** | Managed Kafka (Redpanda) for event streaming | Co-located with workspace region | Event payloads (ephemeral; ingest/normalizer streams only) |

## EU data residency

EU-region workspaces use only EU-resident sub-processors for primary data storage. The few US-only sub-processors above (OpenAI, Braintrust, Resend) are either:

- **Optional / opt-in** — disabled for EU workspaces unless customer opts in
- **PII-stripped** — only operational metadata flows; no customer content
- **Bypassed** — alternate EU-resident services used (e.g., EU-resident transactional email)

Contact [security@sentialabs.ai](mailto:security@sentialabs.ai) for the EU-specific sub-processor configuration.

## Change notification policy

When Sentia adds or replaces a sub-processor:

- **30-day advance notice** to all workspace owners and the security contact on file
- **Update to this page** with new entries highlighted for 60 days
- **Customer objection window** — if a sub-processor change is incompatible with your compliance posture, contact your CSM within 30 days of notification

Emergency changes (e.g., a sub-processor becoming unavailable) may use shorter notice with explanation.

## DPA

Sentia's standard Data Processing Agreement covers all sub-processors. For workspaces with custom contractual requirements, [security@sentialabs.ai](mailto:security@sentialabs.ai) can negotiate addendum language.

## Removed sub-processors

When a sub-processor is removed, customer data they held is deleted per contract within 30 days. Removal events are listed in your workspace's audit log.
