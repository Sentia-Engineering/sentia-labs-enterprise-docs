## Two ways to add people

| Method | When to use | Provisioning speed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SCIM auto-provisioning** | Recommended for any workspace with > 10 users or strict IT requirements | Real-time (driven by IdP) |
| **Manual invite** | Small pilot teams, or before SCIM is set up | Immediate (email link) |

## Manual invite (workspace owners & admins)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Team">
    Press `⌘,` then click **Team**, or visit `app.sentialabs.ai/settings/team`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite by email">
    Click **Invite member**. Paste up to 50 email addresses, comma- or newline-separated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign roles">
    Pick a role per invitee (see the role table below). You can change roles later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Invitees receive a magic link valid for 7 days. They click → SSO with your IdP → land in the workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Invites only work for email domains your IdP is authoritative for. Adding a user from a personal domain will fail at SSO. For external collaborators, see [Guest access](#guest-access) below.
</Note>

## Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full admin: workspace settings, billing, integrations, SSO, data deletion, team management. Cannot be removed except by another owner. |
| **Admin** | Everything except billing and SSO. Manages integrations, populations, simulations, members. |
| **Builder** | Creates and runs simulations, builds populations, connects integrations they own. Most teammates land here. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only. Sees simulations, results, populations. Cannot run simulations or modify config. Good for execs and observers. |

Role assignments are enforced at the API middleware. Every mutation is audit-logged with actor, role, target, and timestamp.

## Bulk invite via CSV

For initial seeding > 10 users, paste a CSV in the invite dialog:

```csv
email,role
ada@example.com,Builder
grace@example.com,Admin
linus@example.com,Viewer
```

Roles must match exactly: `Owner`, `Admin`, `Builder`, `Viewer`.

## Guest access

For agency partners or external consultants, request **Guest** seats from your CSM. Guests:

- Are scoped to specific populations or decisions you grant them
- Can't see workspace-wide settings, billing, or other populations
- Can't trigger calibration writes
- Auto-expire after 90 days unless renewed

## What teammates see on first sign-in

A brief 4-screen welcome:

1. **Welcome to \{your workspace name\}** — explainer + their role
2. **Pick your role focus** — PM / Researcher / Designer / Other (tailors keyboard hints and recommended templates)
3. **Tour** — `S`, `P`, `D`, `I`, `C` nav shortcuts and `?` for the full overlay
4. **First sim suggestion** — Sentia suggests a sample simulation they can run immediately

They can skip the welcome anytime. It's available again from the help menu.
