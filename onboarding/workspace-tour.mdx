Sentia is organized around the design research workflow: bring in design work, define who should react to it, run a study, then preserve the evidence for the team.

| Area | Shortcut | Use it for |

## The first-week mental model

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Figma">
    Figma is the default design source. It powers both direct simulation inputs and Design Agent imports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build one cohort">
    Create a 10‚Äì100 agent behavioral cohort that represents the audience you want to study first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import or describe the design">
    Use Design Agent (when enabled) for Figma-driven variant work, or start a simulation directly with Figma frames or plain text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run and read the report">
    Launch from the 3-step wizard at `/simulations/new`, watch live progress, then read the report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save evidence">
    Save the report or export to PDF (PDF requires the `export-pdf-enabled` flag) for critique, planning, or stakeholder review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

Sentia has six workspace roles. Owners and Admins can invite from **Settings ‚Üí Team**.

| Role | Good fit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Workspace sponsor or operations lead | Full control: billing, transfer ownership, delete workspace. Only an Owner can promote someone else to Owner. |
| **Admin** | Research / design lead or platform owner | User management, integrations, security; cannot manage other Owners. |
| **Operator** | UX researcher, designer, or PM running studies | Builds cohorts, runs simulations, uses Design Agent, saves reports. |
| **Viewer** | Stakeholders and read-only partners | Sees simulation results and reports only. |
| **Billing** | Finance contact | Invoices and payment methods only ‚Äî no workspace data access. |
| **Guest** | External collaborator | Resource-scoped, time-bounded external access. |

For most teams, give active practitioners **Operator** and reserve **Admin** for the people maintaining workspace configuration.

## What is not required on day one

You do not need analytics, calibration, or workflow automation to run the first study. The Day 1 path is Figma ‚Üí population ‚Üí simulation. Release-loop integrations are useful later for calibration workflows.
