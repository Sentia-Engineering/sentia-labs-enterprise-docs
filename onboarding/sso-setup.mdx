Sentia uses **WorkOS AuthKit** for enterprise SSO. Every workspace is corporate-IdP-only — there are no password flows.

## What your CSM configures at kickoff

Your Customer Success Manager provisions SSO with your IT/SecOps team before your workspace goes live. They'll work with you to:

1. **Choose your IdP** — SAML or OIDC. Tested with Okta, Microsoft Entra ID, Google Workspace, OneLogin, JumpCloud, Auth0, PingFederate, and any standards-compliant SAML 2.0 / OIDC provider.
2. **Set up the connection** — WorkOS provides metadata URLs and ACS endpoints for your IT team to add as a SAML/OIDC app in your IdP.
3. **Assign your initial admins** — typically 2–3 users with the `OWNER` role.
4. **Test sign-in** — your CSM validates a sign-in from a test user before handing off.

## Signing in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the app">
    Open [app.sentialabs.ai](https://app.sentialabs.ai). You'll see the "Sign in" page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your work email">
    Sentia looks up your domain → resolves to your IdP connection → redirects you to your IdP for authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete IdP auth">
    Your IdP shows its normal sign-in screen (with MFA if enforced). On success, you're redirected back to Sentia.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land in the app">
    Where you land depends on your role and whether onboarding is complete:

    - **Owner / Admin, first sign-in** → `/onboarding` (the 5-step setup guide).
    - **Operator / Viewer / Billing / Guest** → `/welcome` (workspace readiness summary, then **Enter Sentia**).
    - **Returning users with completed onboarding** → `/overview`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Sessions** are managed via httpOnly JWT cookies, server-side only. Sessions last 12 hours of inactivity; active use extends them automatically. Forced sign-out happens immediately if your IdP deprovisions you (via SCIM).
</Tip>

## Cookies & domains

In production, auth cookies use `COOKIE_DOMAIN=.sentialabs.ai` so the app at `app.sentialabs.ai` and the API at `api.sentialabs.ai` share a session.

- **App:** `https://app.sentialabs.ai`
- **API:** `https://api.sentialabs.ai`
- **Marketing:** `https://sentialabs.ai`

## Common sign-in issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My email isn't recognized">
    Sentia maps your email domain to your IdP connection. If you're using a personal email or a domain not registered for your org, sign-in fails. Use your work email; if that still fails, your IT team may not have finished SSO setup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="IdP redirect loop">
    Usually a misconfigured ACS URL or signing certificate mismatch. Your CSM and WorkOS support can fix it — share the browser network trace with [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I was just deprovisioned and need access">
    SCIM deprovisioning is immediate. Re-enabling requires your IdP admin to re-assign you to the Sentia app. After re-assignment, sign in normally; new access is live in < 1 minute.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="`/api/auth/callback` returns an error">
    Most commonly: the WorkOS redirect URI doesn't match your domain. This is a workspace-level setting maintained by your CSM, not exposed in the Settings UI (the in-app Settings tabs are **General · Team · Integrations · Design system**). Send the browser network trace to [hello@sentialabs.io](mailto:hello@sentialabs.io).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MFA prompt loop">
    This is an IdP-side issue, not Sentia. Your IdP admin can investigate the MFA enrollment status of the affected user.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What SSO does NOT cover

- **API access:** REST API uses JWTs issued by WorkOS but accessed via API keys created in the dashboard. See [API authentication](/api-reference/authentication).
- **MCP server access:** If you've enabled the Sentia MCP server, it uses workspace-scoped service tokens, not user SSO.
- **Webhook signatures:** Webhooks are HMAC-signed per integration; SSO is unrelated.
